ANNOUNCEMENT

Following the Government of Quebec's measures, please note the John Molson Executive Centre’s office are closed until further notice. The team is working remotely and is monitoring the situation carefully in regard to its programs and will consider postponing or cancelling future programs if public health measures are still active or deemed unsafe for our participants. and all activities will be cancelled during that period, except online programs.

 

If you have questions, we are here to help, contact us at jmec@concordia.ca. Please be sure to visit our website and social media platforms frequently for the latest updates. You can also visit the information page to learn more about Concordia University's actions during the COVID-19 outbreak.

 

Thank you for your collaboration.
